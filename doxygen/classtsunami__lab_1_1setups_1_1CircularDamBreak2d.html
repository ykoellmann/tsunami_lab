Detailed Description

2d circular dam break setup.

Initial water height is i_heightInner inside a circle of radius i_radius around (i_centerX, i_centerY) and i_heightOuter outside. Momentum is zero everywhere. The bathymetry is flat by default; an optional isotropic 2D Gaussian obstacle can be added on top.
