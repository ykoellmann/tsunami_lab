Detailed Description

2D artificial tsunami setup.

Bathymetry is a flat ocean floor at -100 m everywhere. The vertical displacement is given by Eq. 5.2.1 on the square [-500, 500] x [-500, 500] m and zero outside. Initial water height and bathymetry follow Eq. 5.2.1.
