◆ setGhost()

virtual void tsunami_lab::patches::WavePropagation::setGhost ( BoundaryCondition  i_left,
BoundaryCondition  i_right 
)
pure virtual

Sets the ghost cell values using independent boundary conditions on the left and right side of the domain.

For a reflecting boundary the ghost cell mirrors the adjacent interior cell: water height (and bathymetry) are copied while the momentum is negated so that the particle velocity at the boundary vanishes.

Parameters
i_leftboundary condition at the left side.
i_rightboundary condition at the right side.

Implemented in tsunami_lab::patches::WavePropagation1d, and tsunami_lab::patches::WavePropagation2d.
